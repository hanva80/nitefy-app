# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Name

NITEFY

## Tagline

Don't guess the night. NITEFY it.

## Brand Positioning

NITEFY is a young, intuitive, visual, and practical nightlife discovery product. It should feel faster and more personal than Google Maps when deciding where to go out.

## Brand Personality

- Young
- Urban
- Clear
- Confident
- Useful
- Visual
- Social in energy, but not dependent on social features

## Tone of Voice

NITEFY should sound direct, modern, and easy to understand. The communication should avoid sounding too corporate or too technical.

### Example Tone

Instead of:

> Discover nightlife venues based on advanced user preference analysis.

Use:

> Find the place that actually fits your night.

## Core Message

NITEFY helps users stop guessing and start choosing the right place for their night.

## UX Principles

1. Make the next decision obvious.
2. Show the vibe before explaining it.
3. Keep the information short and useful.
4. Prioritize location, music, price, dress code, rating, and atmosphere.
5. Avoid unnecessary complexity in the MVP.
