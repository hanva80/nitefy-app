# NITEFY Marketing Launch Plan

## Positioning

NITEFY should launch as a local, visual and music-led nightlife decision tool for Düsseldorf and NRW.

Core message:

> No pierdas la noche decidiendo. Encuentra el sitio que encaja con tu música, vibe y grupo.

The product should not be framed as an AI app or a generic directory. The strongest promise is simple: NITEFY helps users pick the right place for tonight faster than Google Maps, Instagram scrolling or group chat guessing.

## Priority Channels

1. TikTok and Instagram Reels
   - Publish short videos by music taste: Afrobeats, Techno, R&B, Charts.
   - Show real venue expectations: music, atmosphere, dress code, price, queue and crowd.
   - Use these channels as the main acquisition surface for the 18-28 audience.

2. Local microinfluencers
   - Work with nightlife creators in Düsseldorf, Köln, Essen and Dortmund.
   - Use the format: "I tried NITEFY to choose where to go tonight."
   - Prioritize credibility and local taste over large generic audiences.

3. Street and community marketing
   - Place QR stickers, small posters and flyers in campuses, bars, Spätis, barber shops, cafes and club areas.
   - Use copy that matches the immediate decision moment: "Spotify knows your music. NITEFY knows where you should go."

4. Venue partnerships
   - Offer verified or featured presence for clubs, bars and event spaces.
   - Ask for real visuals, fresh event data, discounts or guestlist access.
   - Use partner content to make recommendations more trustworthy.

5. Tonight Match campaign
   - User connects Spotify, selects tonight's vibe, receives a venue match and shares it to the group.
   - This is the main acquisition hook for the MVP.

## Creative Tests

- Music first: "Your music taste, turned into tonight's plan."
- Group indecision: "No more group chat chaos. Pick the right spot fast."
- Venue preview: "Find the place that actually fits your night."

Secondary message variants:

- "Stop scrolling Google Maps. NITEFY the night."
- "Where should we go tonight? Ask NITEFY."

## Measurement

Run the first validation over 2-4 weekends in Düsseldorf.

Track:

- Landing visits
- Spotify connects
- Preference changes
- Venue card clicks
- Venue detail views
- Tester feedback

The winning angle is the one that produces the highest rate of users opening a venue detail page after receiving recommendations.

## Implementation Surface

Keep this plan as an internal launch document until the team decides to expose a public marketing surface.
